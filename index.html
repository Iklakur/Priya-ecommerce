Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can order in two ways: Click the "Order on WhatsApp" button on any product and send me a message, or DM me directly on Instagram at @priyamehta.beauty. I'll confirm your order details, share payment options, and arrange delivery.

Are the products authentic?

Absolutely! I source all products directly from authorized distributors and brands. Every product comes with authenticity verification and original packaging. Your skin's health is my top priority.

What are the delivery charges and timeline?

Free delivery on orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, shipping is ₹50-100 depending on location. Delivery typically takes 3-5 business days for metro cities and 5-7 days for other locations across India.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. Full payment details will be shared once you confirm your order via WhatsApp or Instagram.

Do you offer refunds or returns?

Yes! If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, contact me within 48 hours of delivery and I'll arrange a replacement or full refund. Unopened products can be returned within 7 days for a refund (excluding shipping).

Can you help me choose the right products for my skin?

Of course! Just message me on WhatsApp or Instagram with your skin type, concerns, and current routine. I'll provide personalized recommendations completely free of charge. Your satisfaction is my priority.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, I'll send you the tracking details via WhatsApp or Instagram DM. You can track your package in real-time using the courier's website or app.

Do you ship outside India?

Currently, I only ship within India. However, I'm working on international shipping options. Follow me on Instagram to stay updated on when international orders become available.
